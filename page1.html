Covid makes your brain age faster

‘’A study has found Covid can make the brain age faster and almost increase brain aging by 10 years. Even after you recover from covid you can still face chronic cognitive consequences. In the worst cases people's iq can drop by 8.5 points. “The cognitive function of the participants was not known pre-COVID, and the results also do not reflect long-term recovery so any effects on cognition may be short-term,” Joanna Wardlaw, a professor of applied neuroimaging at Edinburgh University, told Reuters. The research is “intriguing but inclusive,” Derek Hill, a professor of medical imaging science at University College London, told Reuters. “As researchers seek to better understand the long-term impact of COVID, it will be important to further investigate the extent to which cognition is impacted in the weeks and months after the infection and whether permanent damage to brain function results in some people,” he said
